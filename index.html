About The Band

Freshlyground weaves together the rhythmic soul of Africa and the smooth, timeless vibe of afro fusion, creating music that pulses with life. Their sound is a rich blend of earthy tones and electric grooves, where soaring melodies meet deep-rooted harmonies, carrying the stories of a diverse South Africa. For those who seek music that connects hearts and moves bodies, Freshlyground is an invitation to feel, to dance, and to be part of something greater—where every beat speaks to the soul.
